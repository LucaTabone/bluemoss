About

I'm a neuroscientist and professor of neurobiology and ophthalmology and, by courtesy, psychiatry and behavioral sciences at Stanford School of Medicine. My work focuses on brain development, function, and neuroplasticity.

I host the Huberman Lab podcast, where we discuss science and science-based tools for everyday life.
